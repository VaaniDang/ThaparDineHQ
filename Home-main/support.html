FAQ's

This website is a comprehensive guide to the culinary scene around campus. It provides up-to-date menus from all the eateries on campus, making it easy for students to find what they're craving without having to look around or go in person.

The website is very user-friendly. Simply browse the campus map or search for your favourite eating joint.

We strive to keep our menus as up-to-date as possible. We typically update menus once a month, but we may update them more frequently if there are any significant changes.

There are two ways to find the menu for a specific eatery. You can browse the campus map by eatery or use the search bar.

We make every effort to ensure that the prices on the menus are accurate. However, we cannot guarantee that they are always up-to-date. Please be advised that prices may vary.

If you can't find the menu for an eatery, please let us know below. We will try our best to add it to the website as soon as possible.
